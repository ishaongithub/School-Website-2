School Achievements

Mathcounts Competition

This is the time our school formed a science bowl team. It is a competition that spreads across middle school science and math subjects, and they give very hard questions in a few topics for us to guess. This is like a buzzer game, and it is a national competition. Out of 20 students, they held a test and the 5 highest scoring students (Which surprisingly included me) got selected to represent our school. I was surprised that I got in at third place, and in another sub-test which was conducted, I was the fastest out of the people which took it and scored 100% (suprisingly). We had our california regionals a few days ago, and we did not pass. But it was fun.

Science Bowl Competition
